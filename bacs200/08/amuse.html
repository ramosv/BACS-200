My Amusement #1 is FIFA

FIFA stand for the Fédération Internationale de Football Association. It is a non-profit organization and they describe themselves as a international governin body of association football,fútsal, beach soccer, and efootball. It is the highest governing body of football.

Basically every continent in the word has a branch of FIFA. These branches are know as confederations. FIFA's six confederations represent different regions of the world: UEFA (Europe), CAF (Africa), CONCACAF (North and Central America), CONMEBOL (South America), OFC (New Zealand and South Pacific island nations) and AFC (Asia)
